INCOME AND WIFI AVAILABILITY IN NYC

This map shows the relationship between the availability of WiFi hotspots and an areas' median household income at the census
tract level. WiFi hotspots overproportionally serve a higher-income population: whereas New York City's 2015 median household
income (MHI) amounted to $ 59,000, the average MHI of census tracts with WiFi hotspots is $ 72,000.

The map uses data from the NYC open data portal and the 2015 American Community Survey.
